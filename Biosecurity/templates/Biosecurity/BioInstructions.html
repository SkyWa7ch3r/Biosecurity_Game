Biosecurity Instructions

Introduction

Hi, your name throughout the game will be {{ name }}.

Please read through these instructions carefully. If you have any questions please contact one of the administrators. You are about to take part in an experiment about collective decision-making related to risky choices. This experiment gives you an opportunity to earn money. How much you earn depends partly on your decisions, partly on the decisions of others, and partly on chance.

The Basics

The game will consist of 15 rounds with multiple stages per round. You will start the game with ${{ starting_funds }}, each round you will need to pay ${{ upkeep }} for the production of the crops. In the case of there being no incursions from Biosecurity Threats, everyone will have a revenue of ${{ revenue }} and the amount of protection you put towards Biosecurity Threats will be taken away from the revenue.

The maximum amount of protection you can put towards Biosecurity Threats in each round is ${{ max_protection }}. In the case where an incursion occurs, everyone will lose the rounds crops and all revenue is lost, resulting in a loss for that round. The loss will come from the production of crops and the amount of protection you provided those crops against the threat. The chance of incursion will be decided from the amount of protection every group member decides to provide. Even if a majority of the group members provide a relatively high amount of protection against biosecurity threats, an incursion may still occur due to the way the joint probability of incursion is calculated.

How the Joint Probability is calculated:
In a round where there are 2 players and each player contributes an effort of 90% towards protection against Biosecurity Threats to give a 90% chance that they're not the source of the incursion.
Then
Probability of Incursion = 1 - Player 1's Probability x Player 2's Probability
Probability of Incursion = 1 - (0.9) x (0.9)
Probability of Incursion = 1 - 0.81
Probability of Incursion = 0.19 or 19%

In another scenario, again with 2 players, 1 player contributes 90% and another player contributes 10%
Probability of Incursion = 1 - (0.9) x (0.1)
Probability of Incursion = 0.91 or 91%

Group Pledging
In this stage, you will use a slider to decide an ideal target the group should strive for. This target is done on a scale of 0 - 100%, and represents the chance of not having an incursion that the group should strive for. Once you and your group members have individually decided what you believe to be an ideal target (the chance of no incursion as a group), everyone's ideal target will be taken into an equation, which will result in an overall Group Target, the chance of no incursion that your group as a whole has decided to strive for.

Individual Pledging
In this stage, you and your group members will see the Group Target. Using the slider you will pledge how much you will spend on protection against Biosecurity Threats for the next {{ plooper }} rounds. This individual pledge will be shown in currency, below this will be a calculation of probability that you will not be the source of the incursion based upon the amount of protection you are willing to provide. To remind you, this game is based on joint probability, your individual probability is not an indicator of the group's probability of incursion, even if other players do the same amount of protection. Keep this in mind when making an individual pledge (if you're cooperative, you want to pledge above the Group Target to help ensure the group meets its target).
